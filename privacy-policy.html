Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

BabyTrackie ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users and their families. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our iOS application ("App"). Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

BabyTrackie is designed with your family's privacy in mind. All data you enter in the App (such as feeding logs, sleep records, diaper changes, and growth information) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Photos or media from your device
  • Contacts or calendar information

2. How We Use Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device, we do not process, sell, share, or have access to any of the information you enter into the App. The data is solely used to power the App's features on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use Apple's standard iOS frameworks and APIs. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information on how Apple handles data.

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

4. Data Security

Because your data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on the security of your device. We encourage you to use a device passcode, Touch ID, or Face ID to protect access to your device and the data within the App.

5. Children's Privacy

BabyTrackie is intended to be used by parents and caregivers to track baby-related activities. The App does not collect any personal information from children. Parents and caregivers are responsible for the data they enter about their children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
